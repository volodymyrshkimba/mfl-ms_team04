About the Game

"Merge Farm Life: Mansion Story" combines farming and merging gameplay in an engaging adventure where players restore and decorate a countryside mansion. Merge tools, plants, and decorations to create your dream farm and unlock beautiful areas of the estate. Each merge takes you closer to revitalizing the mansion and filling it with life, while fun quests and charming characters make every moment unforgettable.
